6 Science knowledge scale description

Science knowledge is measured on an 18-item measure (O’Reilly and McNamara 2007). The measure contains three sub-scales: Earth Science (7 items), Experimental/Lab Science (6 items), and Scientific Measurement (5 items).

6.1 Variable names

The total science knowledge score is SC0. Experimental/Lab Science is SC1. Scientific Measurement is SC2. Earth Science is SC3.
